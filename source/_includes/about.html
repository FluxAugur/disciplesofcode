About Disciples of Code

Disciples of Code is an instructional blog devoted to teaching the ways of code through a collaborative online process.

As such, we strive to convey our passion, knowledge, and technique to anyone willing to put forth the effort to learn.

We hold to a few mantras as we continuously learn to code.

Less code is better

Nothing is sacred

Make it work, make it right, then make it fast

Understand what you are going to do before you do it

Refactor often

No FUD (fear, uncertainty, or doubt)

Release and build often

Separation of duties is good
